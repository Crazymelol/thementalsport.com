# Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

## Overview
Find where users drop, form a hypothesis about why, and reduce friction or add motivation at that step. Conversion = sufficient motivation minus friction minus anxiety.

## Framework
1. **Map the funnel** and measure step-by-step conversion to find the biggest leak (analytics, session recordings, heatmaps).
2. **Diagnose the leak** — friction (effort/confusion), anxiety (trust/risk), or weak motivation (value unclear).
3. **Prioritize** with ICE/PIE (impact, confidence, ease) — fix the highest-traffic, highest-drop step first.
4. **Apply levers**: clarify value prop above the fold, one primary CTA, remove form fields, add trust (reviews, guarantees, security), strengthen hierarchy, speed up load.
5. **Validate** with an A/B test, not opinion. Measure end-to-end revenue, not just micro-clicks.

## Quick Reference
| Leak symptom | Likely cause | Fix |
|--------------|--------------|-----|
| High bounce on landing | Unclear value / slow load | Sharpen headline, speed, hero |
| Add-to-cart but no checkout | Trust / cost surprise | Show shipping early, badges, reviews |
| Checkout abandonment | Friction / forced account | Guest checkout, fewer fields, wallets |
| Low CTA clicks | Weak hierarchy/copy | One CTA, contrast, benefit-led label |

## Common Mistakes
- Optimizing pages with little traffic to leak data.
- Redesigning everything at once — can't attribute the win.
- Trusting opinions/HiPPO over data and tests.
- Adding friction (popups, mandatory accounts) that kills trust.
- Multiple competing CTAs diluting focus.
- Ignoring mobile, where most traffic and friction live.
- Measuring clicks instead of completed conversions/revenue.
