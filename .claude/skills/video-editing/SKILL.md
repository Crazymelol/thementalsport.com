# Video Editing

## Overview
Editing controls pace and clarity. Cut to keep momentum, hide cuts inside motion, level audio so nothing makes the viewer reach for the volume, and caption everything. The edit serves attention, not the editor's love of footage.

## Workflow
1. **Assembly**: Lay clips in order; rough timing.
2. **Cut on action**: Place cuts mid-movement (a gesture, a turn) so they read as continuous and invisible.
3. **Tighten**: Remove dead air, filler words, pauses, repeated takes. Trim to the fastest version that still breathes.
4. **Audio**: Level dialogue to a consistent loudness (~ -14 LUFS web, -16 LUFS social); duck music under voice ~ -18 to -24 dB; normalize, remove noise/clicks.
5. **Captions**: Generate, sync, and style subtitles; burn in for social.
6. **Export**: Match platform aspect ratio and specs.

## Quick Reference
| Platform | Aspect | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok / Reels / Shorts | 9:16 | 1080x1920 |
| YouTube (landscape) | 16:9 | 1920x1080 |
| Instagram feed | 1:1 or 4:5 | 1080x1080 / 1080x1350 |
| X / general | 16:9 | 1920x1080 |

| Audio | Target |
|---|---|
| Dialogue loudness | -14 LUFS (web) |
| Music under voice | -18 to -24 dB |
| Peaks | below -1 dBTP |

## Common Mistakes
- Cutting on stillness instead of on action (jarring jumps).
- Leaving dead air, breaths, and filler that kill pace.
- Inconsistent levels; music drowning dialogue.
- No captions, or unsynced/auto-garbage captions.
- Wrong aspect ratio for the target platform (letterboxing, cropped faces).
- Over-using flashy transitions instead of clean cuts.
