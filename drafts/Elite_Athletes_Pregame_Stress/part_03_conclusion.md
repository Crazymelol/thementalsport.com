## Chapter 12: Creating Your Pregame Routine

### The 7-Day Protocol

**7 Days Before:**
- Finalize competition plan
- Begin mental rehearsal
- Establish sleep schedule

**3 Days Before:**
- Increase visualization frequency
- Practice breathing techniques
- Minimize decisions

**Day Before:**
- Light physical activity
- Extended visualization session
- Prepare all equipment
- Early bedtime

**Competition Day:**
- Wake up routine
- Nutrition protocol
- Warm-up sequence
- Final mental preparation

### Your Personal Checklist

Create a written protocol that includes:
1. Sleep schedule
2. Nutrition plan
3. Warm-up routine
4. Mental preparation techniques
5. Music playlist
6. Rituals and anchors

## Chapter 13: Practice and Implementation

### Serena's Pressure Practice

Create high-stress scenarios in practice:
- Invite spectators
- Add consequences for mistakes
- Practice with fatigue
- Simulate competition conditions

### Progressive Exposure

Start small and build:
1. Practice techniques in training
2. Use in low-stakes competitions
3. Refine based on results
4. Deploy in high-stakes events

## Chapter 14: The Champion's Mindset

### Long-Term Mental Fitness

**Daily Practices:**
- Morning meditation (LeBron's approach)
- Visualization sessions
- Breathing exercises
- Gratitude and reflection

### Learning from Setbacks

**When you underperform:**
1. Review what happened objectively
2. Identify what you can control
3. Adjust your protocol
4. Practice the new approach
5. Move forward

### Sustaining Peak Performance

**The Marathon, Not a Sprint:**
- Mental preparation is ongoing
- Techniques require practice
- Protocols evolve over time
- Recovery is essential
