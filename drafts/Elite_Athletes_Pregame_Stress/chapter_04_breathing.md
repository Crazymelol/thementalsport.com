## The Power of Breath

Your breathing is the remote control for your nervous system. Change your breath, change your state.

### CASE STUDY: Michael Phelps - The Breathing Techniques

**Phelps' Breathing Arsenal:**

**1. The 4-2-8-2 Technique**
- Inhale for 4 seconds
- Hold for 2 seconds
- Exhale for 8 seconds
- Hold for 2 seconds
- Repeat for improved lung capacity and calm

**2. Lion Breaths**
- Deep inhale through nose
- Massive, explosive exhale (like a roar)
- Releases tension and nervous energy
- Used immediately before races

**When He Used Them:**
- 4-2-8-2: During warm-up and waiting periods
- Lion Breaths: 30 seconds before stepping on the blocks

### The Three Essential Breathing Techniques

**1. Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)**
- Inhale: 4 seconds
- Hold: 4 seconds
- Exhale: 4 seconds
- Hold: 4 seconds

**Use:** General stress reduction, pre-competition calm

**2. 4-2-8-2 (Phelps Method)**
- Longer exhale activates parasympathetic nervous system
- Builds lung capacity
- Creates deep calm

**Use:** Extended warm-up periods, waiting rooms

**3. Lion Breath (Explosive Release)**
- Deep inhale
- Explosive "HA!" exhale
- Releases facial and jaw tension
- Immediate energy boost

**Use:** Final moments before competition
